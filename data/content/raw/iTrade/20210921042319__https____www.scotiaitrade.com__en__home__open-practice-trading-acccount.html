Practice accounts

Try online investing risk free.

Build your investing skills and test our trading platforms without using real money.

Enroll for a Scotia iTRADE Practice Account with a fictional portfolio worth $100,000 CAD and $100,000 USD and you'll be able to try our investing tools risk-free

Features

  • Get a fictional portfolio worth $100,000 CAD and $100,000 USD
  • Access most of the features of a regular Scotia iTRADE account, including trading tools, the latest market research and news
  • Place, modify and cancel market and limit orders for equities and options listed on Canadian and U.S. stock exchanges
  • Reset your account and start over at any time
  • Monitor holdings and track profits and losses in your Practice Account
  • Research and analyze stocks, access current market and economic news, and view sample research reports
  • Integrated with your existing account

How to enroll

Existing Scotia iTRADE clients:

Sign in to the Scotia iTRADE site

  1. Go to the Accounts > Account Summary page
  2. Click on the link to "Enroll for a Practice Account today"
  3. Once you are on the Enrollment page:
    • Verify your email address
    • Acknowledge that you agree to the Terms & Conditions by checking the box
    • Select the "Enroll" button to complete the enrollment process

      • Alternatively, you can go to Manage My Accounts > Additional Services > Scotia iTRADE. Once there, select the "Enroll Practice Account" option and follow the steps above

Scotiabank online banking clients:

Sign in to Scotia OnLine

  1. Go to the Accounts > Account Summary page
  2. Click on the link to "Enroll for a Practice Account today"
  3. Once you are on the Enrollment page:
    • Enter your email address or verify if already provided.
    • Acknowledge that you agree to the Terms & Conditions by checking the box
    • Select the "Enroll" button to complete the enrollment process

      • Alternatively, you can go to Manage My Accounts > Additional Services > Scotia iTRADE. Once there, select the "Enroll Practice Account" option and follow the steps above.

There’s a few benefits of practice trading:

  1. Helps you learn how to factor trading costs and shorts into your strategies.
  2. Gets you comfortable with how to use trading platforms correctly.
  3. Familiarizes you with how price action develops around market fluctuations.
  4. Let's you see how profitability is affected by your trade size.

For new do-it-yourself investors like you, Scotia iTRADE offers an online trading simulator that let you get familiar with the process of trading.
