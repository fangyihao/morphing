All services

For individuals and families with complex wealth management needs who prefer to work with a collaborative team of professionals who can deliver comprehensive solutions.

Investment Management

Together with your input, your relationship manager will provide investment expertise to develop a customized portfolio that reflects your investment objectives and complements your vision.

Learn more, Investment Management

Private Banking

Private Banking provides personalized, attentive and discreet service to help save you time and effort. Our offering features a sophisticated suite of preferential services designed to meet your complex banking and borrowing needs.

Learn more, Private Banking

Scotiatrust®

Together with your relationship manager and specialists at Scotiatrust®, Scotia Wealth Management will help you develop a comprehensive, customized wealth transfer strategy, including a plan for potential future incapacity, centred around your unique financial and personal situation, your goals and your priorities.

Learn more, Scotiatrust®

Total Wealth Planning

The cornerstone of Scotia Wealth Management is a bespoke total wealth plan designed to fit the particulars of your life. This starts with a well-considered analysis that takes into account the various aspects of your life and finances – resulting in a deeper understanding and action plan for your current situation, and a well-informed view to help you make your dreams a reality.

Learn more, Total Wealth Planning
